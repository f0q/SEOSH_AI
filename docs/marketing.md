## One-Line Pitch

**SEOSH.AI** — the all-in-one SEO platform that automates your entire search optimization workflow, from keyword research to AI-powered content publishing.

Stop juggling 10 different SEO tools. SEOSH.AI combines semantic core analysis, AI content generation, SEO optimization, and automated publishing — all in one intelligent platform.

## Key Features

### 🚀 Smart Onboarding
- AI-powered business analysis from your existing website
- Automated competitor research
- Target audience identification
- Intelligent keyword suggestion based on your niche

### 🧠 Semantic Core Engine
- Automated keyword clustering with AI
- Visual keyword-to-page mapping
- Content gap analysis (what pages you need)
- Integration with your existing site structure
- CSV/Excel export

### 📝 AI Content Studio
- Upload photos, notes, video links → get a polished article
- AI-powered content generation optimized for search
- Built-in SEO analysis (uniqueness, keyword density, readability)
- One-click SEO optimization with recommendations
- Markdown editor with live HTML preview
- Content scheduling and publishing calendar

### 🤖 Autopilot Mode
- Set it and forget it — AI generates content on schedule
- Approve/reject via Telegram bot
- Automatic SEO optimization before publishing
- AI image generation for articles
- Configurable publication frequency

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard
- Keyword position tracking
- Search traffic monitoring
- Content performance analytics
- Indexation status
- Integration with Yandex.Metrika, Google Search Console, Yandex.Webmaster

### 🔌 CMS Connectors
- WordPress (REST API)
- Tilda (planned)
- Bitrix (planned)
- Built-in static site generator (planned)
- Custom API connector

### 🌍 Multi-Language
- Full English and Russian interface
- Easy to add more languages

### 🔓 Open Source Ready
- Self-hosted option with Docker
- Bring your own AI models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, OpenRouter)
- Modular SEO tool plugins
- Full documentation

## How It Works (User Journey)

```
1. Sign Up → Create Project → Enter your website URL
   ↓
2. AI analyzes your site, competitors, and industry
   ↓
3. Build semantic core — AI clusters your keywords
   ↓
4. AI identifies content gaps in your site structure
   ↓
5. Create content — upload materials, AI generates articles
   ↓
6. SEO optimizer checks and improves your content
   ↓
7. Publish to WordPress (or schedule for later)
   ↓
8. Track results in Analytics Dashboard
   ↓
9. Enable Autopilot — AI continues creating content
   ↓
10. You focus on your business. SEOSH.AI handles SEO.
```
